What's inside:

Young enthusiasts can keep in touch with each other through our meetings. To achieve this objective, we hold open meetings for everyone to discuss both the technical sides and the more abstract ones of computer science.

What brings us together is the belief that:

  • Access to informations should be free and easily viable through using open source operating systems and not tied to top-down decisions.
  • Users should be aware of what they are using
  • The best way of learning is to share ideas and knowledge, without hierarichical structures and in perfect p2p style.
