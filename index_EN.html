about

mes3hacklab is an interdisciplinary collaboration that provides an open,
accessible community space focused on the sharing of knowledge and hands on learning.
All skill levels, from beginner to expert are welcome to experiment,
develop and innovate in a climate conducive to the spread of know-how in the fields of technology, art and science.
The space is managed and funded by the community with the purpose to facilitate the sharing of skills,
and to contain specialized tools and expertise together in a single location.
We operate at Tuttinpiedi located in Piazza Canova in the heart of Mestre (Venice).
We teach, we learn, we share.
